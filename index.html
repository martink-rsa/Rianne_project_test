About me

I always thought my dream was to own my own restaurant, so I spent 20 years working in restaurants, hotels and bars. But when push came to shove, I realised I wanted something else, something more. Or maybe better said, learn more. My brother motivated me to have a look at coding (which he gravely regrets now that I made him my go to guy for all my questions!) and I am hooked since I started my first tutorial.

A big plus about developing is the freedom to do it from where ever I want. From the hammock on my balcony at home overlooking the mountains, or on a Thai Island so I can go snorkeling when I am finished for the day. It also goes very well with my love for organising anything and everything.

The design part of the job is a necessity for me to make the rest of the code accessible. The real fun, I think, is in the JavaScripts, the servers requests and terminal commands.

For now, I am writing in HTML, CSS (SASS) and Javascript. On my list are Node, Express, MongoDB and Vue. And after that, who knows? The possibilities are endless.
